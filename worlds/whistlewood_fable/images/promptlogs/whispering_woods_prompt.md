# Image Prompt: The Whispering Woods

Create a dramatic, atmospheric scene illustration for a text adventure game.
This is a high-resolution production asset in 16:9 widescreen format.

Location: The Whispering Woods

Scene Description:
A dense forest of ancient, towering oaks whose gnarled bark forms solemn, sleeping faces. To the south, the path leads back to the familiar mossy clearing. To the north, a path of golden leaves leads toward a majestic glowing aura. To the west, a winding trail follows a twist of chimney smoke.
The canopy is so thick that only scattered beams of grey light filter through, creating perpetual twilight. Fireflies drift lazily between the trunks.
Hanging moss and trailing vines create curtains between the trees. The forest floor is carpeted with fallen leaves in faded gold and brown. Mushrooms grow in clusters, some faintly luminescent.
