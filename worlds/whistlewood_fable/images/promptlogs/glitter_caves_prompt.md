# Image Prompt: The Glitter Caves

Create a dramatic, atmospheric scene illustration for a text adventure game.
This is a high-resolution production asset in 16:9 widescreen format.

Location: The Glitter Caves

Scene Description:
A network of underground tunnels, their walls, ceiling, and floor encrusted with glittering crystals of every color. To the north, the rocky passage leads back to fresh air and the brook. To the east, a widening tunnel glows with increasing brilliance.
The passages are narrow and winding, with crystal formations jutting out at unexpected angles. The crystals hum with a faint, harmonious vibration, providing their own soft light.
Tiny stalactites drip occasional drops of glittering water. The air is cool and still, carrying a faint mineral scent.

- Visible pathway ahead: The rocky passage leading back to fresh air and the brook, leading to The Babbling Brook

- Monty the Mole, Excavator, digs frantically at a wall with a tiny silver shovel

Important: These elements should be integrated naturally into the scene, not highlighted
or labelled. They should reward careful observation - exits should look like real
architectural features, items should be placed where they would naturally be found,
and any characters should be positioned authentically within the space.
